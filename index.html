About me

Hello, I'm a frontend-developer. My name is Alexander Rothschild, I'm 27 years old. I have been working with building websites for 7 years now. It all started off during my education as a media graphic designer (2011-2015, KTS). I was taught some basic knowledge about HTML and CSS, and at first I was pretty sceptical, but quickly learned that it was alot of fun! I passionately worked with it since, from projects on my own, projects at the workplace to helping others trying to learn.

I really like the design and creative aspect of frontend. I actually started my education with the dream of becoming a designer. I loved Photoshop and Illustrator at the time, so when I was introduced to a program called Dreamweaver and coding, I was like ... what are these weird codes on my screen? I quickly learned more about it, and it turned out to be a nice combination of things. I could still design, but with code instead of Photoshop. Time went by, and I got more and more into coding. There were so many things to learn and my need for being creative, was still satisfied all the way through.

Coming up to now, I enjoy exploring new areas of frontend as much as possible. I started learning TypeScript some time ago, and I think it's great tool for developers to use. I have been writing JavaScript for a long time (ES5), but never moved on to ES6. I was introduced to TypeScript at my current workplace and been loving it ever since!

I have worked with some frameworks also like ReactJS, PreactJS and Angular (1 and 2). ReactJS is definitely the number 1 for me. It's a great toolbox for a team to use. There is alot of documentation and a big community helping eachother.


TL;DR: I love coding websites and I also have a flair for design.
